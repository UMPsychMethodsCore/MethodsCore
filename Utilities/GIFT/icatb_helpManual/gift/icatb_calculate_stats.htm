Calculate Stats

When the group ICA analysis is run, stats are calculated. The stats are calculated over the number of subjects. If you want to calculate mean or standard deviation image over subjects or sessions or subjects and sessions, "Calculate Stats" entry should be selected under Utilities drop down box. The resulting set of images cannot be viewed in Display GUI and therefore, buttons like Component Explorer, Composite Viewer and Orthogonal Viewer should be used from the main figure window.
